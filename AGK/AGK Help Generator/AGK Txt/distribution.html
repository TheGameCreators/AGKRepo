If you want to help support AppGameKit Studio then please consider the following. This is optional but would be greatly appreciated.

App Notice

Display the following term in either the app loading screen, app credits or app information screen:

Made with AppGameKit Studio www.appgamekitstudio.com

Made with AppGameKit Studio Logo

Use the the official “Made with AppGameKit Studio” logo available for download from this Internet URL; https://www.appgamekit.com/files/made-with-appgamekitstudio-logo.zip

The icon must be kept in the correct aspect ratio and must not be reduced lower than 128x128 pixels.

The logo kit contains images with resolutions varying from 128x128 to 512x512.
